Bequest is a peer to peer service that allows individuals to create wills that are verifiable by a third party. The way it works is simple, sign up as a user of Bequest. Create a will. Both the beneficiary and verifier must be on Bequest as well. Select an amount and your will is created. As a verifier, you can easily verify wills assigned to you. As a beneficiary, collect your bitcoin and be thankful. Easy.
